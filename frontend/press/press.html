Project Overview

Mission

To digitally preserve and present India's rich cultural, historical, and geographical heritage in an interactive, accessible, and engaging manner.

Purpose

Incredible India Explorer serves as an educational open-source platform, allowing users to dive deep into India's diversity through modern web technologies.

Target Audience

Students, educators, travelers, and open-source contributors looking to explore and learn about India's diverse states, festivals, wildlife, and history.

Key Features

  • Interactive 3D maps and timelines.
  • Gamified learning experiences (e.g., monument puzzles, heritage treasure hunts).
  • Extensive offline support and progressive web app (PWA) capabilities.
  • Comprehensive accessibility standards and responsive design.

Brand Usage Guidelines

  • Minimum Spacing: Maintain a clear space around the logo equal to at least half the height of the icon.
  • Do Not Distort: Do not stretch, skew, or rotate the logo. Always scale it proportionally.
  • Do Not Change Colors: Use only the official brand colors provided. Do not apply gradients or outlines.
  • Sufficient Contrast: Use the light logo on dark backgrounds and the dark logo on light backgrounds to maintain WCAG AA contrast.
  • Proper Attribution: When featuring our project, please link back to https://github.com/Eshajha19/Incredible-India-Explorer.
