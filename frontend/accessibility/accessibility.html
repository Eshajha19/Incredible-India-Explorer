Accessibility Statement

Incredible India Explorer is committed to providing an inclusive and accessible experience for all users. We continually improve our platform to align with recognized accessibility standards.

Last Updated: July 2026

1. Accessibility Commitment

We believe that the internet should be available and accessible to anyone. We are committed to providing a website that is accessible to the widest possible audience, regardless of circumstance and ability. Inclusive design is at the core of our development process.

2. WCAG Compliance Goals

To fulfill this, we aim to adhere as strictly as possible to the World Wide Web Consortium's (W3C) Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 at the AA level. These guidelines explain how to make web content more accessible to people with a wide array of disabilities. We perform periodic accessibility reviews to ensure ongoing compliance and continuous improvement.

4. Keyboard Navigation

Our platform is designed to be fully usable via keyboard. You can navigate through links, buttons, and form controls using the Tab key. We provide visible focus indicators so you always know where you are on the page. In complex interfaces, we strive to implement standard keyboard shortcuts to streamline interactions.

5. Screen Reader Compatibility

We have built the Incredible India Explorer with screen reader compatibility in mind. We use Semantic HTML to provide meaningful structure, proper heading hierarchies (H1 to H6), and descriptive alternative text (alt text) for all informative images. ARIA (Accessible Rich Internet Applications) labels are applied to dynamic content and interactive widgets where native HTML falls short.

6. Color Contrast & Themes

All text and interactive elements are designed with sufficient color contrast to ensure readability for users with low vision or color blindness. We do not rely on color alone to convey meaning or prompt actions. Additionally, we support a native Dark Mode which respects your system preferences, providing a lower-glare option.

7. Interactive Map Accessibility

Maps are a core part of exploring Incredible India, and we are working to make them as accessible as possible. Our interactive maps aim to provide:

  • Keyboard controls for panning and zooming.
  • Alternative text descriptions for crucial map data.
  • Accessible legends and interface controls that do not rely purely on pointer events.

8. Known Limitations

Despite our best efforts, there may be some limitations. We are actively working to resolve the following known issues:

  • Some legacy third-party map integrations may lack full keyboard support.
  • A few older user-generated images might be missing descriptive alt text.
  • Highly complex interactive games may not be fully accessible via screen readers yet.

If you encounter an issue not listed here, please let us know.

9. Accessibility Feedback

We welcome your feedback on the accessibility of Incredible India Explorer. If you encounter accessibility barriers, please reach out to us so we can investigate and address the issue.

Report an Issue: Submit an issue on our GitHub repository.

10. Future Improvements

Our journey towards complete accessibility is ongoing. Future planned enhancements include:

  • Implementing more robust screen reader support for all interactive games.
  • Improving map accessibility with richer alternative data representations.
  • Adding additional keyboard shortcuts for power users and those relying on assistive tech.
  • Continuing our ongoing WCAG 2.1 AA compliance audits and remediation work.
