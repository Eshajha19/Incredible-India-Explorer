Attribution Guidelines

Incredible India Explorer aggregates and presents information from highly trusted and authoritative domains. Our content guidelines dictate the following principles:

  • Authoritative Preference: We prioritize information sourced directly from government portals, national institutes, and officially recognized tourism boards.
  • Transparency: Content is synthesized for educational purposes, and primary sources are provided so users can verify information and dive deeper.
  • Citation Standards: Where direct excerpts are used, they are properly attributed in-context with links leading back to the original source.
  • Reporting Inaccuracies: While we strive for accuracy, educational content can become outdated. Users are encouraged to report any inaccuracies so we may correct them promptly.
